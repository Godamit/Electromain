ABOUT US

What is Electroverse Tsec ?

Electroverse.Comm-TSEC, the technical committee of the Electronics and Telecommunication (EXTC) Department at Thadomal Shahani Engineering College (TSEC), Mumbai, has been at the forefront of fostering innovation and collaboration since its inception in 2003. The committee’s mission is to promote interdisciplinary cooperation and hands-on learning experiences, empowering students to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world challenges.Over the years, Electroverse.Comm has organized a variety of technical events, workshops, and competitions that encourage creativity and teamwork. One of its flagship events is the Non-Stop Hardware Hackathon, a unique platform where students from different departments—such as Computer Science, IT, and Electronics—come together to collaborate on hardware and software solutions. This cross-disciplinary environment enriches the participants' problem-solving approaches, allowing them to leverage diverse perspectives and skills.

Coding Weekends

10th August, 2024 - Semester End

Coding Weekend, organized by the Electroverse Committee, is an EXTC-inclusive online coding competition held every Saturday. The event is designed to boost students' coding skills and prepare them for placements, aptitude tests, and internship opportunities. Conducted on a dedicated platform, it offers a competitive yet supportive environment where participants can enhance their problem-solving abilities while receiving valuable career guidance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in the Hackathon?

Any student pursuing a B.E. (Bachelor of Engineering)/B.Tech degree or enrolled in a technical diploma college is eligible to participate.

I submitted incorrect details in the registration form. How can I correct them?

Contact the student coordinators (contact details are provided on the next page) or email electroversecommtsec@gmail.com.

Will food and wireless internet be provided?

Yes, food (lunch, snacks, and dinner) and wireless internet access will be provided throughout the event. However, it is always advisable to have a backup plan.

What should teams bring to the Hackathon venue?

Participants should bring their own devices and any other necessary equipment for the event. Qualified teams must inform organizers about required components after Round 1, which will then be arranged by Thadomal Shahani Engineering College.
